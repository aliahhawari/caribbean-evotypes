PPIE and Community Work

Working with patients, public contributors and community partners to shape this research.

Why PPIE matters

Patient and Public Involvement and Engagement helps make sure our research is shaped by the people and communities it aims to serve. Through discussions, workshops and community events, we listen to what matters most and use this to improve how the study is designed, explained and shared.
