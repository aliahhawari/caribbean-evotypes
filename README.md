# Prostate Cancer Study Website

This repository contains the source code for the 'Prostate Cancer Evotypes for African-Carribean Men study' website.

The website has been developed to provide information about the study, including its aims, background, team, public and patient involvement (PPIE) activities, news, resources, and opportunities to participate.

## About the Study

The study aims to improve our understanding of prostate cancer in African-Carribean men by investigating its genomic characteristics and evolution. By studying tumour DNA alongside clinical information, we hope to identify biological differences that may contribute to disease development and progression, ultimately supporting more personalised approaches to diagnosis and treatment.

## Website Features

* Overview of the research project
* Information about the research team
* News and updates
* Patient and Public Involvement and Engagement (PPIE)
* Resources and frequently asked questions
* Contact information
